Sounds

In Jolly Phonics, the letters sounds are divided into groups of six. They have been ordered carefully so as to aid in learning. The first six letters can be used to make many simple words, such as pin, tip, pan, and sat. This means that it is possible for the children to make words at an early stage in their learning.
